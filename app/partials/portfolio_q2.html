Portfolio - Quake 2 Levels

These Quake 2 levels were developed as a hobby in 1998/99 for use in the massively popular game Quake 2 by ID Software. The levels proved so popular that ID software approached me and asked if they could be included on an official expansion pack for Quake 2.

Download

  • Sepulchre - trdm01a
  • Shadows and Imaginings - trdm02a
  • Tribute - trdm03a
  • Unknown Domain - trdm04a
  • Vertical Asylum - trdm05a
  • Impurity - trdm06

Quake 2 is required to play these levels.
